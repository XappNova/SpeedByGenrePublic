Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 18, 2026

1. Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.

SpeedByGenre operates entirely locally within your browser. It does not have a backend server, analytics trackers, or a database.

  • No User Data: We do not collect names, emails, IP addresses, or browsing history.
  • No Usage Data: We do not track which videos you watch or how long you watch them.

2. How We Use Information

The extension accesses specific data transiently (in-memory) solely to function:

  • Video Category: The extension reads the category text (e.g., "Music", "Gaming") from the YouTube page to determine which speed rule to apply. This data is discarded immediately after use.
  • Settings: Your speed preferences are stored locally in your browser's sync storage (`chrome.storage`) so they persist between sessions. We have no access to this data.

3. Information Sharing

Since we do not collect any information, we do not share, sell, or disclose any data to third parties.

4. Data Security

Because no data is transmitted to us, there is no risk of a data breach on our end. Your settings are secured by your browser's local security sandbox.
