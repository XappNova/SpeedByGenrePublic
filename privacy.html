Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 18, 2026  ·  Applies to: SpeedByGenre Chrome Extension

🛡️ Short version: SpeedByGenre collects no personal data whatsoever. Everything happens locally in your browser. Nothing is ever sent to any server.

1. Who We Are

SpeedByGenre is a browser extension developed by XAppNova. Its sole purpose is to automatically adjust YouTube playback speed based on the video's content category (e.g., Music, Gaming, News). We operate no backend infrastructure and receive no data from users.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We want to be explicit. SpeedByGenre does not collect, store, process, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, IP address, device ID)
  • Browsing history or watch history
  • YouTube account information or login state
  • Video titles, URLs, or viewing duration
  • Location data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic telemetry
  • Analytics or usage statistics of any kind

3. How the Extension Works

To perform its function, SpeedByGenre reads a small amount of data transiently and entirely within your browser. No data ever leaves your device.

Data Accessed Purpose Stored?
Video category text (e.g., "Music", "Gaming") Determines which speed rule to apply to the current video No — read in-memory and discarded immediately
Your speed preferences per category Applies your saved settings on the next YouTube visit Yes — locally in chrome.storage.sync, never transmitted to us

chrome.storage.sync is a sandboxed browser API. Your preferences may sync across your own Chrome profiles via your Google account, under Google's own privacy terms — XAppNova has no access to this data at any point.

4. Permissions We Request and Why

SpeedByGenre requests only the minimum permissions required to function:

Permission Why It's Needed
activeTab + host access to youtube.com To read the video category from the page and control the YouTube video player on the active tab
storage To save and retrieve your speed preferences locally between browser sessions

We do not request access to any other websites, your browsing history, tabs, bookmarks, cookies, or any other browser data beyond what is listed above.

5. Third-Party Services

SpeedByGenre does not integrate with, report to, or load resources from any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking service. There are no embedded SDKs from Google Analytics, Firebase, Sentry, Mixpanel, or any similar platforms.

6. Data Security

Because we never transmit any data, there is no risk of a data breach originating from us. Your speed preferences are stored within your browser's own sandboxed storage and are subject to your browser's and operating system's security guarantees.

7. Children's Privacy

SpeedByGenre does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected at all, there is nothing to disclose under COPPA or similar child privacy regulations.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices — for example, if a future version adds an optional cloud feature — we will update this policy with a new effective date and notify users via the Chrome Web Store listing. Any change that involves data collection will require your explicit opt-in.
